# Pocket-Chinese
Pocket Chinese is a website designed to help international users learn Chinese cooking in a simple and practical way. It also promotes Chinese food culture and explains the logic and philosophy behind Chinese cuisine.

The website focuses on:
- Easy-to-follow Chinese recipes
- Clear ingredient explanations
- Cultural background of Chinese dishes
- The cooking logic and food philosophy behind Chinese cuisine
- Scenario-based recipe categories, such as comforting one-person meals for international students and Chinese dishes suitable for Western-style potluck gatherings, helping users connect food with real-life social and emotional needs

Built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
